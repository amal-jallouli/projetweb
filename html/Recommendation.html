International Institute Of Technology (IIT)

he International Institute of Technology of Sfax (IIT), established in 2012, is a private higher education institution located in Sfax, Tunisia. It offers undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields, including engineering, business administration, and architecture. The institution is recognized by the Tunisian Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research

The International Institute of Technology of Sfax (IIT) offers a dynamic and enriching academic experience for its students, providing a wide range of programs in engineering, business administration, and architecture. The institution's modern infrastructure, including well-equipped libraries, sports facilities, and financial support services, enhances the overall student experience.
